3. machine paradox

experience

The seminar was an interesting experience. From my point of view the machine paradox provides two very relevant aspects to the masters development.

The first and more obvious could be the technical skills you acquire in terms of arduino use in both hardware and software as well as the critical analytical aspect of understanding the industry and how the development of their products is strictly driven by their business model more than any kind of user or sustainability criteria. Which comes as no surprise.

The second and most important aspect, in my opinion, is the actual application of the skills and criteria developed but framed in a more creatively stimulating way. The way the seminar channels the development of the final project allows the researchers to explore and project solutions in different alternative scenarios and create an experience through various uses of technology. From a personal perspective, this is a very interesting way to actually apply a new set of skills.

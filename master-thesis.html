What this studio does

  • Identify one priority site with you (vacant lot, informal corner, or underused public space).
  • Co‑design a simple activation with local people (micro‑park, market, sport or learning area).
  • Support a quick pilot (4–8 weeks) and basic before/after results for funders and citizens.

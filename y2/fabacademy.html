electronics design

The course started with an intense session of one hour fast non stop listing of electronic components and their characteristics on a video conference followed by the participant’s first attempts to sketch a circuit on a pcb.

The overall intention of these classes will be to gain a basic understanding of electronics design and production to at least be able to contribute in a conversation regarding the matter since my understanding of it is very close to zero.

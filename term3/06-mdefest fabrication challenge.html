06. mdefest fabrication challenge

reflection

The challenge was a good opportunity to test the level of engagement the proposal can achieve with the audience. This week was useful to develop a concept around the final exhibition in contrast to the reactions and interactions registered with all the participants along the way and especially during the final presentation.

More than anything this challenge created a space to explore alternative ways to express a whole concept and actually connect with an audience through object interaction. The final proposal still needs to be polished and tailored to be representative of the whole process of the core project being developed in this master. Nevertheless the insights shared with all the participants represent a contribution to the end product.
